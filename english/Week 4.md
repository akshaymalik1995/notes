## WRITE TO ARGUE

The structure of the argument is:
1. Usually, **statement of situation** is given first, followed by arguments for one side against the other.
2. But on third or fourth paragraph, it is **arguments in favour of the other side** and **against the one** stated earlier.
3. Final part usually takes an overview and reaches an acceptable conclusion.

## Essays on Reflection

Steps to write an essay:
1. Do not start writing at once
2. Create a synopsis of relevant points
3. You can also think of examples, illustrations, different kinds of evidence and ideas to further clarify the subject
4. For a good essay, a good synopsis is important.

What is a synopsis?
> A synopsis is an overview, like you stand on the rooftop and look at the entire city or village.

- A good essay for reflection cannot include everything, but it must dwell upon some important things.

- Designing a good synopsis can help you write the best essay you are capable of writing.

- Many of us do not look at all possible aspects of a topic that makes only a partisan essay.

- A good synopsis should quite a comprehensive thought.
