

1. The word interview means ‘a meeting of people face to face, especially for consultation’.
2. All interviews are conversations of a formal nature between two or more people who do not know one another very well.

**Why does an employer want to conduct an interview?**

1. Interview is conducted because the interviewer wants to share his perspective with the candidates that may become an employee in that company and all those on the selection committee may have to work with this person.
2. Before accepting this person only on the basis of some paper certificates, let us also see if that person can speak well, if that person has good social manners, if that person can take surprise questions and occasionally also test this person's knowledge and experience in key areas, that is the precise purpose behind all interviews.
3. An interview is conducted to share expectations, to check fitness of candidates for the job.

**Attributes of successful interviewee:**
1. Confident
2. Polite
3. Good listener
4. Honest/Frank, without being rude
5. Appearance


**Confident**
- You must know your subject well.
- You should know what you are expected to know.


**Polite**
- Respect for the other person, respect for the interviewers.
- This mutual respect shows best in language.
- You must listen carefully, must speak slowly, do not interrupt, do not be distracted, do not feel irritated if at all interrupted, etc.
- Do not allow yourself to be provoked or irritated if sometimes you are asked unrelated questions, provocative questions.
- There are words indicating respect, words like thanks, please, sorry, pardon me, etc.


**Good listener**
- Total attention
- No distraction
- No guessing
- Taking mental note of key points.
- If however, there is a point, or a word, or an expression that for some reason you do not understand, seek clarification.
- Try and understand the question before you answer.
- If you do not understand the question, then do not hesitate in saying, "please pardon me, I do not understand the question."


**Honest/Frank**
- Many more people believe that you need not always be honest, you are unlikely to be caught telling lies or being dishonest.
- By being honest you might lose the present chance, but you may be better prepared for greater success with all other chances.

**Appearance**
- You should know what kind of dress would be considered appropriate for the interview you are going to appear at.
